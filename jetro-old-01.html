    Jetro is senior lecturer in Industrial Production Systems at Linnaeus University (LNU). He is co-responsible for the robotics and automation facility in the SIG (Smart Industry Group) Lab at EPIC Innovation & Technology Center (Växjö, Sweden). SIG is an interdisciplinary research group featuring expertise from computer science and mechanical engineering.

    He is also the thematic area leader for production and production management in the Expertkompetens project for competence development for professionals.

    Jetro has a PhD in manufacturing systems engineering (Luleå University of Technology, Sweden, 2017); he holds a Master of Science MSc degree (University of Twente, the Netherlands) and a Bachelor of Science (BSc.) degree (Anton de Kom University, Suriname.) both in mechanical engineering
