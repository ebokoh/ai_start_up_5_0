About Us

PALM solutions is a company focused on developing innovative AI and embedded systems solutions for a variety of agro industries. Our mission is to create cutting-edge technologies that can improve efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance the capabilities of our customers.


We harness the power of AI and embedded systems to revolutionize the way we grow crops and raise livestock. By utilizing cutting-edge technology, we aim to increase yields, improve efficiency, and reduce waste in the agricultural industry. Our company is dedicated to improving the sustainability and profitability of modern farming practices, while also ensuring the safety and well-being of the food we produce for consumers. We strive to be at the forefront of innovation in the agriculture industry and are committed to working with farmers and industry partners to achieve this goal.
